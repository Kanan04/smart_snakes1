We are students of ADA University and one of our major classes is Principles of Information System, which is taught by Araz Yusubov and Nuraddin Sadili.

From that class we’ve already learned:
  • An introduction to Information systems.
  • How does computer work: hardware and software.
  • What is programming language.
  • What is algorithm.
  • Database systems and applications.
  • Telecommunications and Networks.
  • The Internet, Web, Intranets and Extranets.
  • Information systems in Organization properties does it have.

But this knowledge was not sufficient to develop our own website and that’s why each member of our team first finished individual HTML, CSS, and JavaScript learning from Codecademy. You can see the process of learning on this photo:

Steps of learning:

  1. Learn given theory and get instructions at the end.
  2. Execute your code and fix it if it is incorrect.
  3. Submit the task and get access to the next lesson.

       Then we helped each other to get every instruction and understand them better, we did several practices together, made sure that we all know the rules correctly. Also, one of our mates, Kanan Gubranli, knows these languages better than us, so he helped us to cover some details related with syntax of given languages. The final step of our preparation was choosing the team name and logo. After finishing all preparations and holding the event “Hour of Code”(you can get information about it in the page “Our Goal”), we decided to start creating this website. We used all these three languages, to achieve a high-quality website. As you can see, our website has 4 pages and there are the name and logo of our team and also name of the page at the top of each of them. “Home page” is dedicated for our teammates, so you know us better. On other pages, there exist information about our “Hour of Code” preparations, as we’ve already mentioned, some general information about our team, that you are currently reading, and, moreover, there is form for your feedback and you are welcome to leave your suggestion or critic in that form. We spent our days on this project, tried many versions, colors, sizes to create a visually attractive site. We hope that you, our dear reader, will like it!
